Social Media Content Designs

Designing Engaging Posts with Brand Consistency

Overview

To strengthen the connection between product design and brand presence, I led the creation of social media assets that translated the brand’s visual identity into engaging digital content. The focus was on maintaining consistency across channels while ensuring each design aligned with the broader brand ecosystem.

Using modern design principles, structured layouts, and clear typographic hierarchies, I created content that was both recognizable and impactful. This project demonstrated my ability to adapt a unified brand voice across diverse digital formats without compromising design standards.
